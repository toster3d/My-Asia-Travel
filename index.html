Hello everyone!

I am a traveler who decided to quit my job and start traveling the world.

With my fantastic husband Jakub, we started our journey from Southeast Asia. We spent four months in Thailand, where we visited Phuket, Khao Lak, Phang Nga, Koh Phangan and Koh Samui, Surat Thani, Chiang Mai, Pai, Chiang Rai, Bangkok, Pattaya and Koh Chang.

We then flew to Cambodia where we started our tour in Siem Reap and then headed to Battambang, Phnom Penh, Kampot and Kep.

Our next destination was Vietnam. We spent the first days on Phu Quoc Island from where we flew to Ho Chi Minh City. Then we decided to take the sleeping bus to Mui Ne and Da Lat. The next stops were Hoi An, Ninh Binh, Cat Ba, Sa Pa and Hanoi.

In Malaysia we visited Kuala Lumpur, Ipoh and Penang. From mainland Malaysia we flew to Borneo to the city of Kuching, which was a great base to explore the local nature and national parks.

We finally landed in Indonesia in Bali. We spent some time in Ubud and Kuta. Then we started exploring the island of Java. Our first stop was Banyuwangi, we had the opportunity to see one of the greatest things in our lives - Mount Ijen. Then we went by train to Probolinggo, from where we rented a car to Malang to see the amazing waterfalls nearby. The next stops were Surabaya, Yogyakarta, Cirebon and Jakarta.

It was a very busy 7 months for us, so we decided to go back to Thailand, where we spent the whole month in Krabi, on sweet laziness.

Today we know that traveling is our call in life and we are looking forward to the next trip.
